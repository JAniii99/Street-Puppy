-ABOUT US-

Street Puppy Organization was founded in 2020. Our company is located in Colombo, Sri Lanka. Our orgaization currently employ about 100 people. There are two branches of our organization located in Galle and Kandy.

Our organization mainly produce home for a street puppies, puppies to our coustermers, pet clothes, pet collars, pet toys, pet GPS trackers. Our organization searching for puppies everywhere in the country and provide treatments for them and give them a home untill they find their new home. We always focused on the puppies that we given. We provide special offers for members of our organization and our old coustermers.
